Welcome to Overwatch 2 Top 500 Data.

The data available on this page is not 100% accurate. Data collection involves computer vision, and as such, there is a slight error rate. This error rate is most apparent in some charts where the incorrect role appears, such as a support chart containing Echo. More information on data collection is available on my GitHub page.

What is this data?

The data below is taken from the in-game top 500 leaderboards. The information available there includes a single player's matches played and their top 3 heroes played. The charts and categories below represent data for the hero "slot" in a top 500 leaderboard entry. For example, they count the number of people who have Kiriko as their most played hero, or Widowmaker as their second most played hero. The data is a high-level overview of what heroes are popular or unpopular and is by no means an accurate representation of hero pick rates.

When is this data updated?

The dataset is updated once every week, on Tuesday. The season number is represented by the current season number, and an identifier for the collection number. For example, Season 2.1 would be Season 2, and the first data collection that occurs one week after the start of the season. Season 2.2 would be Season 2, and the second data collection that occurs two weeks into the season. Season 2.8 is the final data collection for the season, eight weeks into the season.
